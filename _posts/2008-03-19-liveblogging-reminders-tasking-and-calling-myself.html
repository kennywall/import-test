--- layout: post title: 'Liveblogging, Reminders, Tasking and Calling Myself' source: http://kinlane.com/2008/03/19/liveblogging-reminders-tasking-and-calling-myself/ domain: kinlane.com image: --- I have been using Jott for that last few months to record tasks, record events, publish blogs, and other general communications by calling Jott and leaving a voice mail or "jott" and it translates and sends to the link that I dictate.

I am pioneering using this for live blogging of events and sending out user generate reporters.

Today I came across Utterz which is a simlar to Jott, but adds in the pictures and videos but utilizes a larger list of links you can post to.

Utterz seems like a true live blogging tool, where Jott seems like a personal assistant and publishing tool.
