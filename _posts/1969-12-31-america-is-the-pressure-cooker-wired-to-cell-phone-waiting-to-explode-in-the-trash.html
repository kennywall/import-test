What is happening this week in NYC concerns me, but I also think it sums up where we are as a country in 2016--a cheap Walmart pressure cooker full of nails from Home Depot, wired to a cell phone, discarded in the trash just waiting to explode.

While we await placement of the next bomb, by the brown people we fear, we do not realize that we are the bomb, pressure building, poised to explode, as soon as we receive the signal via our mobile device.
